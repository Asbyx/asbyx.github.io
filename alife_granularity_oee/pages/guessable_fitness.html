Link with Open-Ended Evolution

Open-endedness in these models often depends on:

  • Ease of reproduction: If too hard, the viable creature space can be very narrow, leading to convergence rather than divergence.
  • The variety of modules and the richness of possibilities allowed by their combinations. Complex systems should still be viable.

Key Concept for OEE: Matter & Information Layers

A crucial aspect, highlighted by projects like ALIEN and inspired by biological evolution (e.g., Smith & Szathmary's "The Major Transitions in Evolution"):

  • Matter Layer: Defines the physics of the simulation, how components interact, their dynamics, etc.
  • Information Layer: Where computation happens, where information about the world can be processed and acted upon.

The decoupling of information (like DNA) from an organism's direct physical structure is a significant trend in evolution. The co-existence and feedback loop between these layers can drastically increase the potential for novel mechanisms and may accelerate the emergence of complex intelligence, which are hallmarks of OEE.
