Link with Open-Ended Evolution

By design, true open-endedness is generally impossible here due to:

  • An explicit fitness function narrowing the search.
  • Often very limited search space for DNA/genotypes.
  • Diversity is not inherently increasing; it converges towards an objective.
  • Major breakthroughs are difficult once a local optimum is achieved.

However, these models are valuable for studying what *not* to do for OEE and for developing metrics to measure it.
